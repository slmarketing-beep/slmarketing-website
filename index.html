About me

Hi, I'm Sjoerd — SEO specialist, builder, and organic growth obsessive.

My SEO journey started with a backpack and a sense of curiosity. Sixteen years ago I set out to explore the world — and that same drive for exploration led me to build Reis-Expert.nl in 2013, which I grew into one of the leading Dutch travel affiliate platforms with over 200,000 unique monthly visitors.

The past four years have been shaped by working in one of the most competitive niches on the internet: iGaming. Here, algorithm volatility is extreme, margins are tight, and only data-driven, technically excellent SEO survives. I've thrived in it — managing two of the largest iGaming affiliate sites and leading a full team of SEO specialists and outreach experts.

Today I take that experience and apply it to businesses across all sectors.
